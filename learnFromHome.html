Make your at home learning experience even better.

01.

Isolation making you feel... well, isolated?

Download a virtual goose to keep you company! It'll walk across the screen, bring you memes, and track mud all over your desktop.

Have a laugh with the Desktop Goose!

02.

Want some digital help in passing your classes?

Download the following apps:
Cymath solves your math problems and gives step-by-step explanations!
Grammarly fixes your spelling and grammar mistakes.
Chemistry and Homework helps you solve chemistry equations.
Cell Biology 101 has practice for your next biology test!
Socratic lets you ask any question and uses Artificial Intelligence to give you an answer.
Scholly Search helps you look for scholarships!

03.

Utilize the following free Google products!

Keep a clean inbox with Gmail.
Organize all your files in color coded folders through Google Drive.
Have something to do? Use virtual sticky notes that you can check off and set times for. Jot it down on Keep.
Online class schedule confusing? Keep track of things with Google Calendar.

Staying organized = staying sane.

04.

You officially have all the time in the world.

Make note taking both fun and colorful by learning basic lettering skills to make your work POP.

Grab some markers and a notebook and make all your virtual classmates jealous with your beautiful notes. Here is a great guide to get started!

05.

Lastly, invest in making a comfortable place for you to work.

To mimic a school environment, try keeping the purpose of your bed to sleeping and find a table/desk to work at.

Making a space that's only for schoolwork will boost productivity and help you focus on the task in front of you. Still remember to take breaks as well!
